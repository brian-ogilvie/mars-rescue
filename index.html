Mars Rescue

Your Mission: Following an equipment failure, humanity's top astrobiologist is stranded on Mars. NASA needs you to pilot the rescue mission. You will have to navigate a treacherous journey, littered with space debris. You will also need to collect fuel floating in space along the way. If you run out of fuel or collide with debris, your mission is a failure. Humanity is counting on you!

Instructions

Control your ship using the arrow keys ⬅️⬆️➡️⬇️.

Dodge any debris objects, shown in GRAY. If you hit one, your ship will explode, and your mission is a failure.

Don't run out of fuel! You will need to collect floating fuel sources, shown in RED along the way, which will replenish your fuel supply.

Your mission is a success when you have successfully piloted all the way to Mars without crashing or running out of fuel.
